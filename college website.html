Academics

NMAMIT offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in the arts, sciences, and professional fields. Our students can choose from a variety of majors and minors, and we offer a variety of interdisciplinary programs that allow students to explore their interests across multiple disciplines.

Student Life

NMAMIT offers a vibrant student life with a variety of clubs, organizations, and activities. Our students are involved in everything from student government to intramural sports to community service. We are committed to providing our students with a well-rounded education that includes both academic and extracurricular experiences.

Our campus is located in a beautiful setting and offers a variety of amenities, including a state-of-the-art library, a fitness center, and a student union. We are a close-knit community and are committed to providing our students with a supportive and welcoming environment.
